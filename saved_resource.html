About

With a combined experience of several decades developing wind and solar projects in the United States, Latin America, and Asia, the consultants at AltoVentus have an unparalleled breadth of knowledge and appreciate the many challenges of getting a project off the ground. We are technical experts in meteorology, environmental modeling, and GIS, teamed up with developers and financial modelers with deep connections and thousands of megawatts of experience in the industry.

At AltoVentus we believe that by leveraging technology, a good project can be successfully moved forward under budget and without large in-house teams. Using the latest software and modeling tools, accurate and professional results are more accessible to project developers than ever before.

Jackson Lord
Principal

Since 2008, Jackson Lord has been on the front lines of renewable power development throughout the U.S. He was a lead analyst on several thousand megawatts of utility-scale wind projects, seeing them through all stages of development. He has managed the data collection for several extensive meteorological measurement campaigns, designed geodatabases to guide the land control strategies across hundreds of thousands of acres, and optimized plant layouts through complex environmental and permitting constraints. He has conducted extensive resource assessments using the latest computational fluid dynamics and array modeling tools, and has interfaced with each of the leading independent engineers.
